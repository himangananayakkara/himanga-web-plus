Our Sweet Story

Founded in 2015, Sweet Bliss Cakes began as a small home bakery with a passion for creating beautiful, delicious cakes that bring people together. What started as a love for baking has grown into Colombo's most colorful cake studio.

We believe cakes should be as beautiful as they are delicious. Our team of talented bakers and decorators work tirelessly to create edible works of art using only the finest ingredients.

Every cake we make is infused with love, creativity, and a touch of magic. Whether it's a birthday, wedding, or just because, we're here to make your celebrations sweeter.
